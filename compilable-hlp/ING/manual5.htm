Appendix A : Mouse emulation

 

If you load a keymap with mouse emulation an icon is added to the taskbar indicating that mouse emulation is active. Once icon appears, mouse emulation remains active even though you close X36Map

The emulation can work at 3 speeds:
  • Step by Step : Press and release.
  • Fast : Press and keep pressed.
  • Very fast : Fast press two or three times and keep pressed.
